Hi there! 👋 Welcome to YiwenYing's Homepage.

I am a junior student at <a href='rcvlab.eee.sustech.edu.cn'>Robot and Computer Vision Lab</a>, from the Department of Electronic and Electrical Engineering in <a href='sustech.edu.cn'>Southern University of Science and Technology</a>, adviced by <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=J7UkpAIAAAAJ'>Hong Zhang</a>.
